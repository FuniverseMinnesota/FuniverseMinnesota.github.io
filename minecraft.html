About Photography 3D Printing Minecraft Projects

I have been a fan of the very popular block game "Minecraft" since I was a child, and have been playing it for over a decade. As an adult I find it a very fun platform for creating third party content such as resource packs and skins. I also enjoy finding and exploring the many communities found within the game.

Minecraft Projects:

ShcortPack NFC - GitHub

ShcortPack is a small resource pack I work on in my spare time for New Frontier Craft, a Beta 1.7.3 mod. It's not quite finished yet but I am still working on it here and there.

Changeable Mining (Bedrock) - Planet Minecraft

Changeable Mining was a small resource pack idea I had in 2022. It's fairly simple and features 5 different block breaking animations that can be selected via a slider in the resource pack menu.

Minnesota's Spleef Arenas (Bedrock) - Planet Minecraft

Minnesota's Spleef Arenas is a small spleef minigame map I made with the help of 2 other people back in 2019. It features a lobby area and 3 seperate spleef arenas. My contribution to the project was the initial planning, part of the redstone functions, majority of the building, and images used for posts of the map.
