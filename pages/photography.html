About Photography 3D Printing Minecraft Projects

I've been doing photography for almost 6 years now, starting when I first took a class offered by the highschool I attended. I like to dabble in all forms of the art but the majority of my work focuses on wildlife and the natural world. Being from Minnesota, many of my photos are taken along the Dakota Rail Trail and locations around it. Hopefully soon I'll be upgrading my camera to the newer Canon EOS R7 and with that Canon's RF lens mount.

A good majority of my work can be found on my Flickr.
